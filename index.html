President & CEO: Joseph Mandour

Mr. Joseph Mandour is a service-disabled veteran, president, and CEO of five companies, among the largest private technology and services integrators in the defense, and government services industry. He is also the founder and chairman of four other businesses in the federal sectors. Mr. Mandour served three years with the US Department of State as a contractor. He also served as consultant to numerous contracting companies before he decided to start his own business in 2016.

Vice President: Daniel Lynam

Mr. Daniel J Lynam is the Vice President of FAR Group, Mr. Lynam is a graduate of American University in Washington D.C. where he studied International Business. Since 2018, Mr. Lynam has been the Vice President of FAR Group INC.

Global Chief Growth Officer David Finch

Mr. David N. Finch is a very talented and accomplished entrepreneur and corporate entrepreneur with over 30 years of large corporate and startup experience. Mr. David N. Finch graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in 1985 with a BA in Economics and Political Science. He obtained his Executive MBA from the University of Richmond, 2002. His experience that heavily originates from starting or being part of starting 7 organizations in his career makes particularly suitable for his position.
